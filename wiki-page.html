The Brown bear

A very dangerous animal!

The brown bear (Ursus arctos) is native to parts of northern Eurasia and North America. Its
conservation status is currently "Least Concern." There are many subspecies within the brown
bear species, including the Atlas bear and the Himalayan brown bear.

The brown bears are the largest of all the bear species in North America.
